Undergraduate Data Science Research Group

Research Overview

Welcome to our research group! We specialize in Data Science (Machine Learning and AI). Our current projects include:

  • Text Summarization
  • Machine learning and Probabilistic Graphical Models
  • Deep Learning Techniques
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Responsible for the engineering of AI
  • Semantic Computing based on Eye-tracking
  • Web Programming and Complex Network Analysis
    •

The group was established in Fall 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic by Dr. Nafa and a group of Computer Science students. The group has participated in the Massachusetts Undergraduate Research Conference and has had formal papers accepted for publication at 8th International Conference on Bioinformatics & Biosciences (BIOS 2022) October 29 ~ 30, 2022, Vienna, Austria , marking the first time Computer Science students have had papers published online.

How to join!

To join the Data Science undergraduate research group, send an email expressing your interest in topics such as Data Science, AI, Machine Learning, Semantic Computing, and Web Programming to Dr. Nafa at fnafa@salemstate.edu Upon receipt of your email, you will receive a welcome message and be introduced to the group members. New members are required to complete a tutorial during their first semester, based on their learning pace. After mastering the basics, members are free to choose a research topic or join a team. The group meets weekly to present progress on research projects, with members who have been with the group for at least two semesters.
