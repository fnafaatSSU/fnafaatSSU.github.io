WiDS at Salem State

WiDS Salem 2023 is an independent event that is organized by Dr. Fatema Nafa Ambassador and Dr. Lakshmidevi Sreeramareddy as part of the annual WiDS Worldwide conference, the WiDS Datathon, and an estimated 200 WiDS Regional Events worldwide. Everyone is invited to attend all WiDS conference and WiDS Datathon Workshop events which feature outstanding women doing outstanding work

We are thrilled to announce that our third virtual(vis Zoom) Women in Data Science event at Salem State will take place on Thursday, June 01, 2023. We hope to see you at the event, and we would be delighted to have you as a speaker next year. Please feel free to share the invitation with your friends, colleagues, or anyone who might be interested!
